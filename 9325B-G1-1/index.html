Bayan Park Village

Content

Security

In order to have a peace and order , the people must feel that they are free from threat or danger.That is why Barangay Bayan Park Village installed a total of four CCTVs in order to monitor their barangay. They are also planning to install seven more CCTVs in order to cover their whole barangay. They also send two brangay tanods together with the police to patrol the area. The barangay tanods are equiped with batuta and flashlight. There is one or two police officers which is sent to patrol for the barangay, this police officer is usually on foot but sometimes they use police mobile. This police officers are equipped with flashlight, guns and handcuffs.

Curfew

Minors - 7:00 am - 7:00 pm
During the said time, children shouln't loiter around public places. If caught, they will be notified, together with the guardian or parents, about the curfew then they will be coming home with the one who caught them to ensure their safety.
Also in this time the owner of computer shop or something the same, shouldn't allow the minors to stay. If the owner got caught, they will get penalized and if caught again they will be out for business

There is a general assembly twice a year to talk about the ordinance.

There are 10 accidents or crime that occurred in Bayan Park Village during the year 2017, which is fewer compared to other barangays here in baguio. Most of this are physical injuries and child abuse. There is also a violation of RA 9165 which is related to drugs on January 2017. There are also Violence against women, unjust vexation, and reckless imprudence resuting damage to properties (RIRDTP). This is why only 2 police officers are being sent in their barangay since crime doesn't take place here often.

Maintaining Order

Bayan Park Village, in order to maintain the order in the barangay, the barangay also implemented the same ordinance that the baguio city implemented. Some of this is the curfew which is already stated above which is City Ordinance No. 50. There is also no parking for the side of the road which can cause traffic since the side of the road is being block making it narrow (BARANGAY ORDINANCE 003-08).
