Vision


We are servant-leaders giving quality service to constituents of Bayan Village Park Barangay and to adjacent barangays.


Mission


To give sincere service to the constituents of Bayan Park Village and to make shape our community the best to live-by.

To be sincere in offering our best service to the best of our ability for the progress/betterment of our Barangay Bayan Park Village.
