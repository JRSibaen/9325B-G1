Maintenance of Main Street



General Cleaning

Bayan Park Village is conducting a monthly cleaning drive every last week of the month. This drive aims to keep the village free of diseases and pests

General Assembly

Bayan Park Village does general assemblies with other barangays in Auraro Hill to discuss issues regarding each village. and also how to maintain the environment in their village. this assembly happens twice a year.

Garbage Collection

The Collection of garbage in Bayan Park Village is every monday at 3:00 am in the morning.

Tree Planting

Bayan Park Village has a project called tree planting project. They plant trees in the village to preserve or help the environment. though they did not tell us where they get the seeds for the planting it could be very well coming from the government or their village funds, either way this movement of theirs will hopefully be mimicked by other barangays in the area.
