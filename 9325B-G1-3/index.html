Maintenance of Main Street

General Cleaning

Bayan Park Village is conducting a monthly cleaning drive every last week of the month. This drive aims to keep the village free of diseases and pests. They used to put garbage cans on the streets but it was being stolen so what they did was remove the garbage cans and conduct cleaning of the streets once or twice a week to keep the streets clean.

General Assembly

Bayan Park Village does general assemblies with other barangays in Auraro Hill to discuss issues regarding each village. and also how to maintain the environment in their village. this assembly happens twice a year. This is like proof that all the barangays in Aurora Hill are working together to keep things in order here in Aurora Hill.

Garbage Management

The Collection of garbage in Bayan Park Village is every monday at 3:00 am in the morning. The ordinance of not littering is being strictly implemented, this is to minimize the garbage that is being thrown on the streets.

Tree Planting

Bayan Park Village has a project called tree planting project. They plant trees in the village to preserve or help the environment. though they did not tell us where they get the seeds for the planting it could be very well coming from the government or their village funds, either way this movement of theirs will hopefully be mimicked by other barangays in the area.

Migrating to GREEN

Bayan Park Village is already starting the say no to plastic ordinance. This ordinance aims to remove all plastic bags in the village and convert to paper bags. This is to help the environment to lessen the use of plastic in their area. Anyone caught using plastics in their stores will be given a warning, if they do it again there will be a penalty, in the fourth time that the owner got caught will have his or her business shut down by getting their business permit.

Discussion

The Capitan said that "Barangay Park Village is only upholding and implementing the rules of Baguio City". Although they are only implementing the already existing rules in their barangay, this can greatly impact the people that are living in the area. Since the rules are directly specified in their area, they are most likely to follow the rules
