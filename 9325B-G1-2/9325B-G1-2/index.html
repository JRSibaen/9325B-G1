Susbtance Abuse Prevention and Rehabilitation

The power of drugs to influence not only the present but also the picture makes it all the more disturbing. Drugs target youth in their most productive years. As a consequence, drug abuse can easily convert a vibrant source of productivity into a burden society.

Illegal drugs place serious constraints on the ability of developing nations, like the Barangay, to acheive improvement accross like the full range of established development goals.

Addressing the direct and indirect impact of illegal drugs on the development is imperative for the Barangay to succeed in its pursuit of inclusive growth and sustained Baranagay Development.
