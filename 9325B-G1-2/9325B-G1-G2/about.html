Brief History

Basing on the facts and informations we gathered covering the history of Bayan Park Village, Bayan Park was named after the late Sergio Bayan, who was the first Mayor of the City of Baguio. During the early days when Aurora Hill was formely known by pioneers as the Workingmens' Village, there was a time that Bayan Park merged as one barangay together with Leonila Hill Baranagay.

It was in these circumstances that Bayan Park emerged in the political system as an essential basic vehicle in the realization of political normalization in the country. Thus, daring the Martial Law of the late President Ferdinand E. Marcos that Bayan Park and Leonilla Hill were separated into two different barangays. At present, Bayan Park now belongs to the 7 barangays of District IX, which is one of the two districts of Aurora Hill and among the 129 Barangays of the city of Baguio. It is bounded on the North by Ambiong Baguio Barangay; on the South by West Bayan Park and San Antonio Village; and on the west by East Bayan Park and South Central Aurora Hill.
