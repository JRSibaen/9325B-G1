- MELVIN A. CORTES

Punong Barangay

Bayan Park Village Anti-Drug Plan of Action 2017-2018

Activity Expected Output Office/Person Responsible Target Benificiary Financial Requirement Timeline
Intensify Barangay anti-drug operation to address emerging drug trends and patterns related to production, processing,trafficking and retailing of dangerous drugs Strengthen Barangay clearing operations through active involvement of anti-abuse councils at the barangay Barangay council, PDEA , PNP Barangay Constituents 1 000 PHP -tarpaulin 1st and 2nd quarter
Strengthen involvement in the drug advocacy by organizing more capacity building programs for youth, parents, transport personnel, teachers, senior citizen, media and medical practitioners and other professionals. Expanded enforcement measures in identification of drug syndicates and watch lists. Informed and educated in programs will shall serve in the treatment intervention particularly on the implementation of a drug free Barangay.
