Substance Abuse Prevention and Rehabilitation

"Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance you must keep moving.” – Albert Einstein

The clear and imminent danger of being involved in drugs either as

a user or a pusher has never been more fatally realistic than today.

A war has been waged and as a result the society in general is made

aware of the horrors of what it can do to the community.

Drugs target the most basic unit of the society, the family. Families

are being destroyed and being torn apart because of drugs. The

youth in particular are the ones that are most vulnerable to these

attacks. It robs our children of the right to live in a safe and healthy

community.

The barangay can help the nation gain development and achieve

success on its war against drugs by addressing the clear and imminent

threat of the direct and indirect effects of drugs, with its sustained

development and solid and effective campaign.
