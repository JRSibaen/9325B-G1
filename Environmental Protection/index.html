Vision


A Barangay in which every child should attain the right to survival,protection,development and participation.


Mission


To implore breakthroughs in a way the barangay treats children and to achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives.


Values


We take personal responsibilities for using our resources efficiently, achieving measurable results and being accountable to supporters, partners and most of all children.


Ambition


We are demanding ourselves and our colleagues, set high goals and are committed to improving the quality of everything we do for children.


Collaboration


We respect and value each other,thrive on our diversity and work with our partners to leverage our global strength in making difference in children.


Creativity


We are open to ideas,embrace change, and take disciplined risk to develop sustainable solutions for our children. We aspire to live to the highest standards of personal honesty and behavior. We never compromise our reputation and always act to the best interest of the children.
